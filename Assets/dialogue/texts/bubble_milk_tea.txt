You found the bubble milk tea!
Bubble milk tea is a tea-based drink that originated in Taiwan in the early 1980s.
Taiwanese immigrants brought it to the United States in the 1990s.
Bubble tea most commonly consists of tea accompanied by chewy tapioca balls.
In Taiwan, bubble tea has become not just a beverage,
but an enduring icon of the culture and food history for the nation.
Now bring it to the world!
